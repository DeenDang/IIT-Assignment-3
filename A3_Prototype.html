Project Description

Overview

With the development of current technology, the application of medical software plays a very important role in the health care system. Our project was born as a solution to the impact and urgency of the epidemic, which is in hospital personnel management and electronic medical records for the health care system. This application can help improve the hospital-patient connection and strengthen the hospital. 

Many hospitals continue to use paper-based records because they believe it is the quickest way to manage patient care. When it matters most, essential information regarding health is either unavailable or lost in the midst of emergencies. However, if they use electronic information storage software, they can easily access the patient's records with a few simple taps on the screen. Patients can also view their health status and prescriptions after the examination. In addition, we include a working hour management feature to help nurses and doctors understand everything about their shift that day and who works with them.

Motivation

The idea of our project was born from a complaint from nurses working in a local clinic about scheduling workdays and electronic health records. The nursing staff can't see who works with them, which is very inconvenient for them when they want to contact the people who work with them the same day to exchange work schedules or change shifts. And more importantly, almost all medical records are recorded on paper, not online. So it takes a lot of time for the doctor to plan the treatment or when the patients go to another clinic or hospital, the doctors and nurses there cannot see the previous medical records. Therefore, our project will improve the healthcare system in a convenient way. Doctors/Nurses can understand more about the patient's health condition and doctors/nurses can easily interact with each other with the common goal of conveying good quality of medical services best to everyone.

Our project is mainly about digitising data which is one of the top 10 technology trends in 2022 according to the Forbes magazine "Data is a key enabler for all of these trends". Working on this project gives me many opportunities to land a job in big technology corporations in software and data such as Google, Amazon, Slack software or in software companies in health technology as systems Administrator, Cyber Security Analyst in “Why Information Technology Important in Healthcare” by National American University

Detailed Description

Aims

The aim of our project is to create a software application with focus on the doctor’s appointment schedule, patient details, medical department collaboration, resources and payrolls. It will be a native application in which both employees and employers will be able to use to track time on shift. And, to track incoming patients as well as patients treated and to be treated by the logged in employee. Through this application, the employee will be able to access all patient personal and diagnostic information. In addition, pharmacist can access the patient’s records right after doctor upload the information, reduce the time patient needs to wait for and increase the efficiency of picking the medication with less obstacles. The development of this application could streamline hospitals and improve hospital connection to patients. It could also result in fewer medical errors because the information needed for each patient would be uniform and clear. Furthermore, by continue this path, we want to create an integrated system based on this software, including the aspects of doctor patient communication, real-time monitoring, medical logistics collaboration, after treatment services, etc.

This application was created because of the impact that this pandemic causes on the healthcare system. The construction of this application. This application was created to simplify hospital operations and strengthen the interaction between hospitals and patients. The uniformity and clarity of the information required for each patient will also prevent medical mistakes. This programme was created by our development team with an international humanitarian approach. And is dedicated to the efficient use of existing medical resources for system integration and management so that most patients may be efficiently treated.

During this project, some small goals has been established. For example: main functions for doctors, communication with other departments, resources, payrolls and other features currently won’t be able to express. Because we’re making an application starting with doctors, so we are putting the achievable functions like appointment displaying, patient info & records, departments, schedules, payments, other resources and personal details & payrolls as in high priority. The appointment displaying is the most important function for doctor to visualise and modify the patient’s appointments. In patients’ info & records, the doctors could check the patient’s personal info, medical details, health records and other relate to the patient. In departments function, the doctor could communicate with other departments inside the hospital, this could use as an emergency or general communications with a message and email system. Schedules is a visualised display of appointments and tasks that needs to be done, with a clear, simple visual design and comes with small functions like see details when press into the specific task and could also add tasks with manual if necessary. Payments function is a simple record for any medical payments like Medicare and insurance. In the resources function, we add the section publishing the latest articles in medical field that is relate to the current logging doctor’s field and it is customisable. Also, the doctor could save the articles they like and subscribe the favourable journals for their preference. These categories that has been described will be achieving throughout software creation and they will be containing in the project artifact / potential shippable artifact.

Plans and Progress

At the beginning of the project, our group's idea was to develop a simple medical software that doctors could use. It would have features such as being able to see what appointments and other work doctors have, having a freely adjustable weekly schedule, being able to view their salary, being able to view their clock-in and clock-out records and patient appointment information and medical records. The project idea didn't live up to the teacher's claim of having innovative software or games or anything IT related. So, we started adding new features to the original one, which include the ability for doctors to contact and communicate with patients during treatment, the ability for doctors to contact and communicate with doctors in other departments, the ability to monitor in real time the patient's medication and physical condition during medical treatment, the assurance of the latest medical situation and access to the latest medical academic articles. These ideas were also slowly filtered out by our team to try to ensure that the software did not have too large and complex a system, which would otherwise make the development project very difficult and ignore the core purpose of the system by covering too many elements. From the development side, adding too many elements would have made the project extremely difficult and slow, so we removed some early ideas, such as: the users of the software would include doctors, nurses, patients, pharmacists, senior management, medical logistics and the pharmaceutical supply chain. There were too many points to focus on because of the wide coverage.

After talking about the extent covered by this project, the next step for our group was to figure out what kind of development software tools would be used for this project and the ability of the group members to implement these software features. In the group, we listed some of the tools needed to develop the software, including Java, JavaScript, C language, Swift, Python, SQL (in terms of database), and some features that may be needed such as NFC technology. Since our group had no experience in developing software, we could only discuss what programming language to use to write but did not actually apply the tools. In the follow-up process, when we needed to implement some features, we used figma, a tool for developing and presenting user interface features in a mobile simulation on the web. With this software, we were able to create some mobile user interfaces that included some of the features that we proposed. Of course, this page is only in the simulation of the mobile environment, the actual code is difficult for us to implement, with the help of this platform to help us make something that can be used for physical display. During the experiment phase, we have put forward the function and user-friendly interface did come out one by one, including the booking record and list, task lists, patient information and records, other medical departments, week schedule, patients with health insurance and insurance payment records, other medical doctor related resources and personal account Settings and system Settings. The important functions that we have selected represent the core purpose of our initial software project, which is to implement simple clock logging and patient interaction functions for doctors and other ancillary medical functions.

The last screen is resources, which is not as high a priority as the previous ones, but we thought it would be a good feature to put there. Scope and Limits

Through this application, staff will have access to all patients' personal and diagnostic information.

The system will also keep track of the patient's prescriptions or medical treatments, as well as their dosages. Even if the nurse who administered the medicine to the patient is no longer there, the application will include all the required information to deliver the medication. Medications and past dosages given to the patient will be accessible to the patient in the facility and team using the system, allowing the patient to get the most up-to-date medical care.

Our project will feature two user interfaces, one for physicians and nurses and the other for patients to see physicians' comments on prescription medicine orders.

Our idea will not only assist the medical staff in getting to know the patients, but it will also assist the patients in getting to know the medical personnel, so that they become better acquainted.

A simple chat system will be integrated into the application.

Doctors may respond to patients' urgent inquiries and make emergency connections. This function only permits basic typing and the transmission of speech videos and images; it does not support the transmission of location information, emoticons, etc.

Testing

Our project will be evaluated in four parts. During this procedure, we will conduct user testing to verify that the application is intuitive enough for users of all skill levels.

First, we will do unit testing to validate functionality, test it independently, and search for faults in areas of the project that can be tested separately. This involves ensuring that each page's functionality is implemented.

Integration testing then follows, during which the separate software components are combined and tested as a whole. Assembled and evaluated as a unit. For example, our program's full usual operation is functioning well.

System testing follows, which comprises crucial tests such as functional testing, user interface testing, interface testing, stress testing, reliability testing, and security testing. We will investigate the market's numerous operating system types, including Windows, Mac, Unix, and Linux, to see whether they can execute this test.

Acceptance testing is the last step of this process; it assesses if the software fulfils the functional requirements provided in the project requirements. Requirements. The purpose of content testing is to verify the correctness, accuracy, and relevancy of the offered material. The correctness, precision, and relevancy of the presented information.

To test our applications, we include user testing into our teaching sessions wherever feasible. We may ask students to fill out a questionnaire about the usability of the user interface and the flow of the sites, as well as any other complaints or feature ideas, so that we may modify our features based on user input.

Skills and Jobs

Information Architect

An information architect would be essential in building a software where it is heavily based on users especially where there are multiple types of users to cater for such as in our project. Information architects work with stakeholders as well as the front end software engineers to create a front end that suits the clients needs while also having a well laid out user centered interface. Skills that this person would need to be fluent in are a good understanding of User Centered Design and Client facing communication. They would need to have great computer skills and be efficient in such programs such as Visio, Photoshop and other designing and/or wire framing softwares. They also would need to be efficient in excel and possibly group collaboration softwares such as Trello and Jira. They would likely be very innovative and creative in their thinking as they are not only working towards the clients goal but also catering the users wants and needs. Someone like this would be essential in the set up of our software management system.

Marketing Technologiest

A marketing technologist is someone that is able to help to guide a companies digital marketing strategies in a unique way where they can aid in implementing the software required. Software Engineer / Programmer

Software Engineer responsibilities include gathering user requirements, defining system functionality and writing code in various languages, like Java, Ruby on Rails or .NET programming languages (e.g., C++ or JScript.NET.)
