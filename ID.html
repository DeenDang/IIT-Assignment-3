How do the IT-specific skills in your required skill set rank in terms of demand from employers?

IT-specific skills are always among the most important for employers. If the job is a software/website development engineer, employers will be looking for candidates who can code in multiple languages, as most software, systems, and websites are created in multiple languages. combined language. Developers and programmers should understand HTML, C, Java, JavaScript, Python. For workers working in the cybersecurity and networking industry, they have a good understanding of computer networks and how it works. Cybersecurity engineers require at least knowledge about Networking Security, Cloud Security, Coding (C/C++, Python, SQL,etc), Operating System. Finally, System Administrator must know about system administration, Virtual Machine
