Assignment 2


Team: Group 4

IT Work

Topic: Interview an IT professional

1. Please tell us about your IT work. What exactly do you do?

IT Technical Support Officer, help staff solve network and computer issues.


2. Please tell us about the industry you work in.

MCRI, research institute.


3. What other kinds of work do you have to do?

Answering phone calls.


4. Who are all the different people you interact with in your work? Please tell us about them.

Only colleague and the staff in research Center and hospital.


5. Please tell us about your interactions with other IT professionals.

We have team meetings with other group, really depends on the IT issue.


6. What about your interactions with clients or investors?

Did not have any interaction with clients.


7. What aspects of your work do you spend most time on? Please tell us about these.

Answering staff phone calls, and remotely solve the issues.


8. Which aspects of your work do you find most challenging?

Answering phone calls because you do not know whether you can resolve it or not.


9. Finally, can you share an example of the work you do that best captures the essence of the IT industry?

Staff cannot access network drive, it can be the issue of VPN, can be the issue of network setting, lots of possibilities, and we help to solve it.


From this interview, we can see that the IT practitioner interviewed, her daily work is to help colleagues in the same organization to solve IT-related problems, mainly through telephone to communicate with colleagues, because only for the same company so do not need to communicate with guests. She also does some research on the feasibility of IT projects and then lets other groups implement the project.
