Assignment 3


Team: Group 4

Project Idea: Healthcare System

Overview

Our project will be to aid the healthcare system, it will be a native application in which both employees and employers will be able to use to track time on shift. It will be able to track incoming patients as well as patients treated and to be treated by the logged in employee. Through this application, the employee will be able to access all patient personal and diagnostic information. Also, the system can record what prescription or medical treatment that the patient needs and the dose of the medication for their treatment. In addition, pharmacist can access the patient’s records right after doctor upload the information, reduce the time patient needs to wait for and increase the efficiency of picking the medication with less obstacles.

Motivation 

The burden the epidemic has placed on the healthcare system is what spurred development of this application. The development of this application could streamline hospitals and improve hospital connection to patients. It could also result in fewer medical errors because the information needed for each patient would be uniform and clear. Furthermore, this application help contain and display the records of the patients’ medical treatment and medication they need, this is transparent for doctors, nurses, pharmacists, and patient themselves. In addition, a simple chat system will be integrated into the application, this could bring the relationship between doctors and patients closer, and doctor can answer some urgent questions from patients and for emergency contact.

Nowadays, the level of medical care varies from region to region, with some areas having developed and overflowing medical resources, while others have a lack of medical conditions, and some residents cannot even receive effective treatment or even examination. Our development team has developed this software with an international humanitarian vision, and is committed to the efficient use of existing medical resources for system integration and management, so that the majority of people can receive effective medical treatment and help. We believe that all people are equal before life.

Description 

This application will be able to track employees clocked time in and any overtime accrued over the period of their shift. The staff will also be able to keep track of any patients who are coming in, as well as those who are already on their rounds and those who need to be treated. To guarantee that all information is accurate and that everyone can comprehend the situation with more effective communication, all diagnostic and personal information, in addition to this kind of patient information, will be accessible via the application.

Patients will be able to receive medicine prescriptions, transfer to other doctors or nurses, or be recommended to them. The required information such as dosage could be automatically sent to the hospital's pharmacy to be dispensed and then sent to the nurse so that this can be given to the patient. The system becomes useful in the way that even if the nurse aiding the patient is now different, the application will have all the required information to administer the medication to the patient and any previous doses given the patient.

Patients can be provided with the most up-to-date medical solutions at the facilities and teams that use the system, without switching hospitals and residences. The system can integrate all registered medical resources, which can greatly improve the efficiency of resource utilization and reduce the waste of medical resources. It is also easier to integrate different patients' conditions for future research on different symptoms and viruses, and these data will be fed to the registered medical facilities and related teams in real time.

The system will also help the registered medical team, the system will be authorized by the medical staff to test, and the implementation of uploading to the database and analysis, each doctor and medical facility staff will get real-time physical health and mental data. Especially when dealing with highly contagious diseases and symptoms, the system will frequently compare patients with their first testers. It also provides feedback on the mental status of registered staff, for example, if a doctor is under stress, the system will give him/her a rest or recuperation program with minimal disruption to medical efficiency.

The system will use a grading system to rate different registered medical facilities and their staff from big data to promote the progress of all registered medical facilities. Doctors in the higher segments will have priority over excellent medical and research resources to realize the potential of the best doctors. Physicians will be rated by a number of metrics such as attendance, case completion, case symptom difficulty, case success rate, etc. Salaries and benefits for physicians at different levels will be matched to their capabilities.

At the same time, to ensure the best interest of all hard-working medical facilities. The system will analyse the average income and expenses of the residents in each year and customize the consumption packages for different groups of people for different consumption levels, and I am willing to provide customized packages to serve customers with special needs. The more advanced members will receive more medical resources, as well as priority treatment rights. Our software is dedicated to providing the best medical and other related services to all people, and every user is our most important customer.

Skills

The skills that would be required to complete this project could be fulfilled by all members of the group. Depending on the platform decided for the project, proficiency in either Swift, Java would be required to build the application. We would also need someone to build a database that is capable of handling large amounts of data and can expand as required by the enterprise, as well as someone with skills in cloud design and cybersecurity as a lot of personal information would be kept within this system from patient information as well as employer information.

Outcome

If this project is successful, this will help doctors, nurses and patients improve the treatment process. Firstly, we will use Trello to plan our project in detail and update the progress of each step and still use Microsoft Team for communication. All members will post any new idea or trouble for everyone discuss and have meeting every week. Secondly, we will do some research and survey about our project’s interface and use Figma for design and conceptualise the interface of our tracking application. There will be two interfaces (one for Doctor and Nurse and other for patients to see reviews from doctor about medicine prescriptions). Java and JavaScript will be the main language of this project and we choose to deploy the project on React-Native which is an open-source software UI development kit, so the app can be used for both Android and IOS operating system. SQL is used for data storage (medicine prescriptions, personal profile of doctor, nurse and patient, detailed treatment process) and NFC (Near Field Communication) for tracking location technology.
